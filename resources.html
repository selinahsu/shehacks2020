Protecting your Home

If you live close to a rural, forested area, there are a number of extra considerations you may need to make in advance.

  • Close all windows and doors in the house.
  • Move indoor combustible materials such as light curtains and furniture away from the windows.
  • Move outdoor combustibles materials away from the house, including firewood, fallen leaves, and lawn furniture.
  • Turn off gas and electricity.
  • Cover vents, windows, and doors with duct tape.
